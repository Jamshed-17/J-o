### **Figma**

**1. Name of image editor:** Figma  
**2. Is it a type of Raster or Vector Graphics?** Vector  
**3. Is it proprietary or free editor?** Free with paid plans  
**4. Supporting OS:** Web-based, macOS, Windows, Linux  
**5. General description:** A powerful, web-based design tool focused on user interface and experience design. It is known for its real-time collaboration features.  
**6. Image editing possibilities:** Primarily for creating and editing vector shapes, prototypes, and layouts. Basic raster image placement and cropping are supported, but not advanced photo manipulation.  
**7. File support:** .fig, .svg, .png, .jpg, .pdf
